# Money Roadmap

This roadmap is documentation only. It does not change payment, PayOS, ads, analytics, Firestore, or production settings.

## North Star

`truyen24h.vn` should become a real Vietnamese online fiction business asset with safe automation, reader growth, and monetization.

## Revenue Pillars

- Organic reader traffic.
- Registered reader accounts.
- Coin top-ups.
- Paid chapter unlocks.
- Monthly VIP.
- Author/creator earnings.
- Ads and affiliate placements after policy-safe setup.
- AI-assisted content operations with quality controls.

## Safety Gates

Owner approval is required for:

- production deploys
- Firestore rules deployment
- PayOS live changes
- payment, payout, refund, or bank changes
- env and secret changes
- ads or affiliate account changes
- mass publishing automation
- high-risk revenue automation

## Practical Priority Order

1. Secure auth, admin, payment, coins, VIP, unlock, withdrawal, and rules.
2. Confirm production smoke checks.
3. Improve VIP and coin conversion UX.
4. Add revenue and payment health reporting.
5. Add safe content and SEO automation.
6. Add controlled growth experiments.

## Non-Goals For Phase 0.1

- No payment implementation.
- No PayOS settings changes.
- No ads or analytics configuration.
- No runtime revenue code.
- No deploy.
